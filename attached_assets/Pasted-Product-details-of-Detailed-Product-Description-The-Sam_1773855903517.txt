- Detailed Product Description

The Samsung Galaxy S24 Ultra is the pinnacle of Samsung’s flagship smartphone lineup, bringing cutting-edge technology, a superior camera system, and unparalleled performance. Built for users who demand the best in every category—whether it’s photography, performance, or display quality—the S24 Ultra delivers an all-around premium experience. Below is a comprehensive breakdown of the specifications and features of the Galaxy S24 Ultra:

---

1. Design & Build:

Dimensions: 162.3 x 79.1 x 8.6 mm

Weight: 234 grams

Materials:

Front: Corning Gorilla Glass Victus 2 (durable protection for drops and scratches).

Back: Corning Gorilla Glass Victus 2.

Frame: Armor Aluminum frame for added durability.

Colors: Phantom Black, Phantom White, Green, and Burgundy.

The Galaxy S24 Ultra showcases a premium design with sleek lines, curved edges, and a robust yet elegant build. The polished metal frame and matte glass finish create a sophisticated look while ensuring durability.

---

2. Display:

Size: 6.8-inch Dynamic AMOLED 2X display

Resolution: 1440 x 3088 pixels (~500 ppi density), providing extremely sharp visuals.

Aspect Ratio: 19.3:9

Refresh Rate: 120Hz adaptive refresh rate, which adjusts between 1Hz and 120Hz based on the content, ensuring smooth scrolling while conserving battery life when appropriate.

Brightness: Up to 2,500 nits peak brightness, making it one of the brightest displays in the market, ensuring excellent visibility under direct sunlight.

HDR Support: HDR10+ support for enhanced color and contrast in supported content.

Always-on Display: Yes, offering glanceable information like time, notifications, and more.

Protection: Corning Gorilla Glass Victus 2 for strong protection against drops and scratches.

---

3. Performance:

Processor: Qualcomm Snapdragon 8 Gen 3 (4 nm)

Samsung’s choice of Qualcomm’s latest and greatest chipset ensures top-tier performance in AI tasks, gaming, and overall system fluidity. It excels in both speed and energy efficiency.

GPU: Adreno 750 for improved graphics performance, delivering a smooth experience in gaming and high-demand applications.

RAM: 12GB LPDDR5X RAM, providing ample multitasking capabilities for power users who switch between apps frequently or use demanding applications.

Storage: 256GB UFS 4.0 (non-expandable), which offers ultra-fast read and write speeds for loading apps and files quickly.

---

4. Cameras:

Rear Camera System:

Primary Camera:

Resolution: 200 MP, f/1.7

Sensor Size: 1/1.3", 0.6µm pixels

Optical Image Stabilization (OIS): Yes, ensuring stable photos and videos even in motion.

Laser Autofocus: Yes, providing precise focus in low-light and fast-moving scenarios.

Ultra-Wide Camera:

Resolution: 12 MP, f/2.2

Field of View: 120°, ideal for capturing wide landscapes or group shots.

Periscope Telephoto Camera:

Resolution: 10 MP, f/4.9

Optical Zoom: 10x optical zoom

Super Resolution Zoom: Up to 100x, allowing you to capture details from a distance with Samsung’s Space Zoom technology.

Telephoto Camera:

Resolution: 10 MP, f/2.4

Optical Zoom: 3x optical zoom for versatile mid-range zoom photography.

Key Camera Features:

8K Video Recording: Up to 30fps, offering ultra-high-definition video capture.

4K Video Recording: Up to 60fps with HDR10+ support for dynamic range and color.

Nightography: Samsung’s enhanced night mode for clearer, more vibrant shots in low-light conditions.

Expert RAW: Enables users to shoot in RAW format for professional-grade photography.

Super Steady Mode: Improved video stabilization for shake-free video recording.

Front Camera:

Resolution: 12 MP

Aperture: f/2.2

Field of View: 90°, wide enough for group selfies or vlogging.

Key Features: Dual Pixel autofocus, 4K video at 60fps, AI-driven portrait enhancements, and face retouching options.

---

5. Battery:

Capacity: 5000 mAh, providing all-day battery life even with heavy use.

Charging:

Wired Charging: 45W fast charging (able to reach 50% charge in around 30 minutes).

Wireless Charging: 15W wireless charging (Qi-certified).

Reverse Wireless Charging: 4.5W reverse wireless charging, allowing you to charge accessories like Galaxy Buds or other devices.

Battery Saving Features: Adaptive Power Saving mode that optimizes power based on usage patterns.

---

6. Software:

Operating System: Android 14 with One UI 6.0

One UI 6.0 Features:

Personalization: Enhanced customizations with deeper color palettes and theme options.

Multitasking Improvements: Split screen and floating windows for improved productivity.

Samsung DeX: Turn your Galaxy S24 Ultra into a desktop-like experience with Samsung DeX.

Bixby Routines: AI-powered automation that adapts to your habits, offering custom shortcuts for apps and features.

Updates: Samsung promises 4 years of major Android updates and 5 years of security updates.

---

7. Connectivity:

5G Support:

Supports Sub-6 GHz and mmWave 5G networks for ultra-fast download and upload speeds.

Wi-Fi: Wi-Fi 7 (802.11be), ensuring faster internet speeds, low latency, and better coverage.

Bluetooth: Bluetooth 5.3 with A2DP, LE for superior wireless audio quality and quick device pairing.

NFC: Yes, for contactless payments and other NFC-related functionalities.

USB Port: USB Type-C 3.2, enabling faster charging and data transfer.

SIM Card: Dual SIM (Nano-SIM and eSIM), supporting multiple network connections.

---

8. Security:

Fingerprint Sensor: Ultrasonic in-display fingerprint sensor, offering fast and reliable unlocking even in wet conditions.

Face Unlock: Secure face recognition for an alternative biometric unlock method.

Samsung Knox: Multi-layered security platform that protects sensitive information from malware and external threats.

Secure Folder: A dedicated space on your device for extra-sensitive data.

Updates: Samsung offers 5 years of security patches, ensuring your device stays protected from the latest threats.

---

9. Additional Features:

IP Rating: IP68 water and dust resistance, protecting the phone from splashes, spills, and submersion in up to 1.5 meters of water for up to 30 minutes.

S Pen Support: Built-in S Pen with improved latency for smooth handwriting and drawing. It’s ideal for note-taking, sketching, or even controlling the device remotely.

Stereo Speakers: Tuned by AKG, delivering rich and immersive sound with Dolby Atmos support for a surround-sound experience.

No 3.5mm Headphone Jack: Audio is delivered through the USB-C port or via Bluetooth.

---

10. Box Contents:

Samsung Galaxy S24 Ultra

USB-C to USB-C cable

Quick start guide

SIM ejector tool

No charger included, following Samsung’s sustainability initiative to reduce electronic waste.
